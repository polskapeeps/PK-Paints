Tailored trim that finishes your home

From classic crown moulding to modern feature walls, we design, fabricate, and install custom millwork that matches your architecture and ties into newly painted spaces. Every profile is cut, fitted, and finished on site for seamless results.

  • Design support, material selection, and layout mockups before any cuts are made
  • Seamless integration with our painting crews for perfectly finished trim and panels
  • Meticulous installations that include caulking, filling, sanding, and finishing touches

Architectural mouldings

Crown, casing, baseboards, and chair rails cut on-site for tight joints and clean transitions between spaces.

  • Traditional and contemporary profiles
  • Column wraps, beams, coffers, and ceiling accents
  • Seamless caulking and paint-ready finishing

Built-ins & storage

Maximize every inch with custom shelving, media centers, mudroom lockers, and window seats built to your exact dimensions.

  • Cabinet-grade construction with adjustable shelving
  • Integrated lighting, outlets, and soft-close hardware
  • Paint, stain, or specialty finish options to match adjacent rooms

Feature walls & accents

Transform blank walls into focal points with board-and-batten, shiplap, slat walls, fireplace surrounds, and statement staircases.
